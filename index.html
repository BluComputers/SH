About Sarah

Hello everyone and welcome to my website! The home of Bo and Obs! And also the rest of my poetry.

I started writing after my mum died really as grief therapy. Looking after my dad through the day and writing at night. I write about anything that happens to drop into my head so maybe here you'll find something that strikes a chord.

I want my poems to be available to anyone so if you do see one you'd like to use for any occasion there's no need to ask me. Here and now I give my blessing. Just dont say you wrote it!! Thanks for dropping by.

Love Sarah H ❤️
